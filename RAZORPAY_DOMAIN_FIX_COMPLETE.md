# Razorpay Live Keys Not Working - Domain Registration Fix

**Date**: April 9, 2025  
**Issue**: Live keys blocked - domain not registered in Razorpay  
**Solution**: Register domain in Razorpay dashboard

---

## 🔴 PROBLEM

Your Razorpay live keys are not working because:

```
❌ Domain www.gvplacements.com is NOT registered in Razorpay
❌ Razorpay is blocking payments from unregistered domains
❌ Error: "Payment blocked as website does not match registered website(s)"
```

---

## ✅ SOLUTION

You need to manually add your domain to Razorpay's whitelist.

---

## 📋 STEP-BY-STEP FIX

### Step 1: Log in to Razorpay Dashboard

1. Go to: **https://dashboard.razorpay.com**
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click: **Sign In**

---

### Step 2: Navigate to Website Settings

1. Click the **Settings** icon (gear icon) in the top right
2. From the dropdown menu, click: **Website & App Settings**

---

### Step 3: Add Your Domain (WITH www)

1. Look for the section: **"Websites"** or **"Registered Domains"**
2. Click: **Add Domain** or **+ Add Website**
3. Enter: **www.gvplacements.com**
4. Click: **Add** or **Save**

---

### Step 4: Add Your Domain (WITHOUT www)

Repeat Step 3 for the domain without "www":

1. Click: **Add Domain** again
2. Enter: **gvplacements.com** (without www)
3. Click: **Add** or **Save**

---

### Step 5: Verify Your Domains

After adding both domains:

1. Razorpay will send verification emails to your registered email
2. Check your email inbox (and spam folder)
3. Click the verification link in each email
4. Verification takes 24-48 hours

---

### Step 6: Wait for Approval

1. Razorpay will review your website
2. They check for:
   - Professional design ✅
   - Clear business information ✅
   - Privacy policy ✅
   - Terms & Conditions ✅
   - Contact information ✅
3. Approval takes 24-48 hours
4. You'll receive an email when approved
