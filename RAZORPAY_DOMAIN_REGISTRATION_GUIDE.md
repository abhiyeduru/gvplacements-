# Razorpay Domain Registration - Step-by-Step Guide

**Goal**: Add your domain to Razorpay to fix the "Payment blocked as website does not match registered website(s)" error

**Time Required**: 5 minutes to add domain + 24-48 hours for verification

---

## 🎯 QUICK OVERVIEW

Your website is live at: **https://www.gvplacements.com**

Razorpay is blocking payments because this domain is not registered in their system.

**Solution**: Add the domain to Razorpay's whitelist.

---

## 📋 STEP-BY-STEP INSTRUCTIONS

### Step 1: Log in to Razorpay Dashboard

1. Go to: **https://dashboard.razorpay.com**
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click: **Sign In**

---

### Step 2: Navigate to Website Settings

1. Click the **Settings** icon (gear icon) in the top right
2. From the dropdown menu, click: **Website & App Settings**

---

### Step 3: Add Your Domain

1. Look for the section: **"Websites"** or **"Registered Domains"**
2. Click: **Add Domain** or **+ Add Website**
3. Enter your domain: **www.gvplacements.com**
4. Click: **Add** or **Save**

---

### Step 4: Add Domain Without WWW

Repeat Step 3 for the domain without "www":

1. Click: **Add Domain** again
2. Enter: **gvplacements.com** (without www)
3. Click: **Add** or **Save**

---

### Step 5: Verify Your Domains

After adding both domains:

1. Razorpay will send verification emails to your registered email
2. Check your email inbox (and spam folder)
3. Click the verification link in each email
4. Verification takes 24-48 hours

---

### Step 6: Wait for Approval

1. Razorpay will review your website
2. They check for:
   - Professional design
   - Clear business information
   - Privacy policy
   - Terms & Conditions
   - Contact information
3. Approval takes 24-48 hours
4. You'll receive an email when approved

## 🔧 TROUBLESHOOTING

### Issue: "Domain not found" error

**Solution**:
1. Make sure you entered the domain correctly
2. Check that domain is active and pointing to Firebase
3. Wait 24 hours for DNS propagation
4. Contact Razorpay support

### Issue: Verification email not received

**Solution**:
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Wait 5 minutes and refresh email
3. Request verification email again from Razorpay dashboard
4. Contact Razorpay support

### Issue: Still getting "Payment blocked" after approval

**Solution**:
1. Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
2. Try in incognito/private mode
3. Try on different browser
4. Contact Razorpay support
