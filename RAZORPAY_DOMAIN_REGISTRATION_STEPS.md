## 🚀 STEP-BY-STEP INSTRUCTIONS

### STEP 1: Open Razorpay Dashboard

**Action**: Go to https://dashboard.razorpay.com

**What you'll see**:
- Razorpay login page
- Email and password fields

**What to do**:
1. Enter your email
2. Enter your password
3. Click "Sign In"

---

### STEP 2: Go to Settings

**Action**: Click the Settings icon (gear icon) in the top right corner

**What you'll see**:
- A dropdown menu appears
- Multiple options including "Settings"

**What to do**:
1. Look for the gear icon (⚙️) in the top right
2. Click it
3. Select "Settings" from the dropdown

---

### STEP 3: Find Website Settings

**Action**: Look for "Website & App Settings" or "Websites"

**What you'll see**:
- Settings page with multiple sections
- Look for "Website & App Settings" or "Registered Domains"

**What to do**:
1. Scroll down to find "Website & App Settings"
2. Click on it
3. You should see a section for adding domains

---

### STEP 4: Add First Domain (WITH www)

**Action**: Click "Add Domain" or "+ Add Website"

**What you'll see**:
- A text input field
- "Add" or "Save" button

**What to do**:
1. Click "Add Domain" button
2. Enter: **www.gvplacements.com**
3. Click "Add" or "Save"

**Important**: Include the "www" part

---

### STEP 5: Add Second Domain (WITHOUT www)

**Action**: Click "Add Domain" again

**What you'll see**:
- Another text input field
- "Add" or "Save" button

**What to do**:
1. Click "Add Domain" button again
2. Enter: **gvplacements.com** (without www)
3. Click "Add" or "Save"

**Important**: Do NOT include "www" this time

---

### STEP 6: Check Your Email

**Action**: Check your email for verification links

**What you'll see**:
- 2 emails from Razorpay
- Each with a verification link

**What to do**:
1. Check your inbox
2. Also check spam/junk folder
3. Open each email
4. Click the verification link in each email
5. Wait for verification (24-48 hours)

---

### STEP 7: Wait for Approval

**Action**: Wait for Razorpay to review and approve

**What happens**:
- Razorpay reviews your website
- They check for professional design
- They verify compliance pages
- Takes 24-48 hours

**What you'll see**:
- Approval email from Razorpay
- Confirmation that domain is approved

---

### STEP 8: Test Payment

**Action**: Once approved, test the payment

**What to do**:
1. Go to: https://www.gvplacements.com/razorpay-test
2. Fill test details
3. Click "Test Payment ₹1"
4. Use test card: 4111 1111 1111 1111
5. Complete payment
6. See success message

## ❓ TROUBLESHOOTING

### "I can't find Website & App Settings"
- Look in Settings menu
- Scroll down to find it
- It might be under "Account Settings"

### "I didn't receive verification email"
- Check spam/junk folder
- Wait 5 minutes and refresh
- Request verification email again

### "Still getting domain error after approval"
- Clear browser cache
- Try incognito mode
- Try different browser
- Contact Razorpay support

### "Payment still not working"
- Verify domain was approved
- Check that both domains are added
- Verify email links were clicked
- Contact Razorpay support
